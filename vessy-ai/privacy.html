Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Vessy OS stores and processes account and conversation data to operate the service. By using the site, users understand that their account information, chat history, and settings may be stored for functionality, continuity, moderation, and service improvement.

Information collected

  • Account details such as username, optional email, and password hash
  • Chat history associated with the signed-in account
  • Settings such as personalization choices and background preferences
  • Uploaded files or images when the user sends them to the service
  • Technical session data used to keep users signed in

How information is used

  • To provide account access and save conversation history
  • To operate personalization features when enabled by the user
  • To moderate abuse, protect the service, and enforce restrictions
  • To improve product quality and reliability

Cookies and local storage

Vessy OS uses cookies or browser storage for sign-in state, consent preferences, settings, and other core functionality. Optional advertising technologies may be added later if the site becomes monetized.

User controls

Users can log out, delete their account, and delete learned personalization data from the interface where available. Disabling personalization stops new memory data from being added through that feature.
