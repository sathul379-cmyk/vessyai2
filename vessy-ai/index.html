Original AI Workspace

Vessy OS helps people chat, create, organize ideas, and stay in one place.

This site combines AI chat, uploads, voice tools, account history, and private sessions inside one glass-style workspace. It is built for everyday users who want one assistant that feels fast, simple, and personal.

What Vessy Does

Vessy OS is designed for natural chat, file-based help, image understanding, account memory, and a clean conversation workflow. It is intended to be useful for study, planning, writing, ideation, and lightweight productivity.

How Content Is Managed

User sessions are account-based, chats are stored privately for the signed-in user, and moderation controls can be used to remove access or restrict abusive accounts. AI outputs are educational and should be reviewed by the user.

Who It Is For

This product is aimed at students, creators, small teams, and curious users who want a single AI interface for questions, drafts, summaries, brainstorming, and simple media actions.

Features

Core product areas

Chat and memory: Ongoing conversations, saved history, and optional personalization.
File and image support: Upload files or images and ask Vessy to analyze the content directly.
Voice tools: Voice replies, voice call mode, and browser-based speech fallbacks.
Account controls: Login, settings, logout, account deletion, and admin restriction tools.
